STATS:

Attack: Attack modifier added to weapon damage
MAttack: Magic attack modifier added to spells
Defense: Flat damage reduction applied to damage taken
Armor: damage reduction modifier applied to damage taken
Hit: Attack rating (accuracy)
Flee: Dodge rating (evasion)
ASPD: Attack Speed of physical attacks
Precision: Weight applied to top of weapon damage range
Crit: Chance to deliver a critical hit
Modifier: Damage modifier applied to critical hits
